Capacitance Sensing Workshop

Capacitive Sensing is a technology, based on capacitive coupling, that takes human body capacitance as input. This workshop will introduce Capacitive Sensing and cover several commercial products for implementing Capacitive Sensing in your projects and/or products.

Workshop Objectives :

  • Understand Basic Capacitance Measurement
  • Understand Capacitance Sensing Algorithms
  • Understand Physical/Layout of various control surfaces, such as buttons, sliders, and wheels
  • Hand's on Labs with several different types of typical Capacitance Sensors

Workshop Length :

  • One Hour for Introduction/Overview
  • One Hour to Three Hours for Hand's On Labs
  • Total : 2 to 4 hours

Required Hardware :

  1. Windows Compatible Computer with USB port
  2. Capacitance Sensing Breakout Boards
